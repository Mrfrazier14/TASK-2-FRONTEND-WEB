Did You Know?

  • Nevada is the driest state in the United States, with an average annual rainfall of only about 7 inches.
  • Las Vegas, Nevada, is known as the "Entertainment Capital of the World" and is famous for its casinos and nightlife.
  • The state's official nickname is the "Silver State" because of its significant silver deposits.
